About

Let me introduce myself.

Hi! My name is Vikas and I am a senior at Dublin High School and a member of the Dublin Engineering and Design Academy (DEDA). I am fascinated by computers, electronics, and mechanical design and I have gained an appreciation for the various disciplines of engineering through my high school courses and extracurricular activities. I aim to work more specifically with artificial intelligence and robotics to hopefully help push the boundaries of technology in this world.

I discovered my passions for engineering through challenging classes, school clubs, internships, and opportunities from DEDA. I’ve been a part of Gael Force Robotics for three years, advancing from a lead builder and programmer in 2018 and 2019 to captaining my own team in 2020. Robotics became a timeless escape from reality and allowed me to develop a love for creating elegant and efficient designs for mechanical and software problems. Despite the season being cut short due to COVID-19, I’m proud of our accomplishments of elite competition rankings and the growth of my leadership. I am also the co-founder and vice president of the Dublin Science Olympiad team, where I managed administrative work and the finances of a $6K+ budget. I led and competed in physics/engineering division events which involved making rubber-band propelled model aircrafts, structurally efficient balsa wood boomilevers, precise distance travelling mousetrap-powered vehicles, mulltifarious Rube-Goldberg machines, high hangtime parachutes launched from soda bottle rockets, and complex circuit analysis. I got to work on more delicate and precise projects which often kept me up late at night with cyanoacrylate glue, epoxy, and Exacto knife cuts all over my hands. I am also a member of a competitive CyberPatriot team, where I specialized in creating automated scripts and securing operating systems vulnerabilities in Linux computers.

More recently this summer, I interned at the cybersecurity company Cog Systems under the supervision of my DEDA mentor Rupert Young. Cog Systems specializes in securing mobile and IoT devices with embedded software and type-1 hypervisors. I worked on solutions to virtualized driver splitting in the Android Linux kernel for Cog’s Virtualized Mobile Device as well as secure mobile VPN connections.

Thanks for visiting my website and feel free to look through my projects or contact me with any questions, comments, or concerns.
